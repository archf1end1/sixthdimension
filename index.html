MISSIONS

Unlike ISS or other Space Station whose main objective is to act as a hub for research, on the other hand Colosseum provides all kind of facility from assembling whole rocket in space(Our main mission is to assemble our own state-of-the-art rocket in space) to 3D printing medicines and from acting as fuel station for rockets(Starship need multiple refueling in earth orbit to go on) to storage facility for storing future payloads.

Our second but most import mission is to assemble our full state-of-the-art rocket in space inside our Colosseum. In future it will get more and more difficult to make bigger rocket in earth and launch from the earth sufrace but we will need a ship which has capabilty of shiping heavy weights by assuming less fuel which is only possible if we start building/assembling rockets in space. On earth gravity is biggest challenge but in space it is the biggest plus point. From there we can easily launch ship for a long journey

Our third mission is to build critical infrastucture on the moon which will act as an base for others to achieve what they want.
